    What is a visitor management system?
    A visitor management system is a software solution that helps organizations manage and track visitors, including their check-in/check-out times, purpose of visit, and other details.
    Why is visitor management important?
    Visitor management is important for ensuring security, enhancing operational efficiency, and maintaining compliance with regulations. It helps organizations keep track of who is on their premises and for what purpose.
    How do visitors sign in with VisitorOn?
    Visitors sign in with VisitorOn by using a touchscreen kiosk to enter their details. They can also sign in using a pre-registration link sent via email or SMS.
    How can I use a visitor management system to help in emergency evacuations?
    A visitor management system can help in emergency evacuations by providing real-time data on who is on-site, which can be used to ensure that everyone is accounted for and safely evacuated.
    How does a visitor management system help with compliance?
    A visitor management system helps with compliance by maintaining accurate records of visitors and their activities, which can be useful for audits and ensuring adherence to industry regulations.
    How do I create a visitor management system?
    Creating a visitor management system involves defining the requirements, selecting the appropriate technology, developing the software, and implementing it within your organization. You may also consider using pre-built solutions that can be customized to meet your needs.
    What features should I look for in a visitor management system?
    Look for features such as visitor check-in/check-out, badge printing, integration with existing systems, reporting capabilities, and support for multiple languages and accessibility options.
    Why is a cloud-based visitor management system useful?
    A cloud-based visitor management system is useful because it allows for easy access from any location, ensures data is backed up and secure, and can scale with your organization's needs without requiring extensive on-premises infrastructure.
    Does a visitor management system help with security?
    Yes, a visitor management system helps with security by tracking who enters and exits the premises, verifying visitor identities, and providing alerts or reports on suspicious activities.
