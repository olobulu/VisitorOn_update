Effortless Visitor Registration for Hospitals and Clinics

A Professional First Impression for Your Healthcare Institution

  • Customize VisitorON to match your healthcare facility’s identity with personalized branding options
  • Instantly notify doctors, nurses, and administrators of visitor arrivals via the VisitorON Pocket app
  • Ensure patient safety with photo capture and printed visitor ID badges for all guests

    • Why VisitorOn is the best fit for your hospital, clinic or healthcare center

    Visitor Screening

    Screen all visitors entering your premises with a simple yes/no question. This can trigger an alert to the host or another specified contact like your health & safety manager.

    ID Badge Printing

    Easily identify visitors in the building by their visitor badge.

    Data Privacy and GDPR Compliant

    All visitor data is securely stored and the VisitorOn system is fully GDPR and data privacy compliant.

    Contact Tracing

    VisitorOn time-stamps every visitor and employee in and out. Discover easily who was on the premises should you need to carry out contact tracing.

    Visitor Sign In

    Easily track additional people who are entering the facility.

    User Friendly

    With a range of people using the system—from patients to visitors—it’s a user-friendly setup, simple and easy to use for anyone.

    Improve Healthcare Safety, Save Staff Time, and Create a Professional Sign In and Out Experience

    Our healthcare visitor management system keeps track of who has signed in and out, where and when. This provides a digital timeline which is accessible for historic reporting and compliance needs. The modern sign-in software saves valuable time for administrators, and its intuitive interface and automated processes ensure a hassle-free experience for patients, staff, and visitors alike.

    With its paperless and environmentally friendly sign-in process, ID badge printing, visitor screening questions, contact tracing, and built-in data compliance, VisitorsON's healthcare visitor management system is ideal for numerous applications. From hospitals to clinics, healthcare providers worldwide are streamlining and reshaping their facility operations with VisitorsON.
